Welcome to Christchurch's premier gallery for the visual arts.

Our love for creative work stems from curiosity in the world around us – and especially in its visible beauty. The Art Works Gallery seeks to nurture space for artistic endeavours. We believe that learning is a natural part in both birthing community, as well as welcoming people, from all cultures and backgrounds.

We believe that our collection in the visual arts forms a pātaka, not simply of unique history here in New Zealand, but also of human culture at large. It involves crossing boundaries and creating new relationships. So we do this by showing both local and international pieces, as well as both acclaimed and unfamiliar works, with care and appreciation.
